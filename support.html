Support

We're happy to help.

Contact

Email support@galaspo.com for help with setup, streaming issues, or anything else about GalaSpo Stream. We try to reply within a couple of business days.

Before you email us

  • Stream won't go live? Check that a schedule exists for today's game in the Schedule tab, and that your YouTube account is connected in Settings.
  • Video looks soft at higher resolutions? Make sure your connection can sustain the bitrate for that resolution — the app automatically steps the bitrate down under a weak connection, which can affect quality.
  • Team names or logos not showing? These come from the schedule you created for the match — double-check they were saved there.

Feature requests & bugs

Send those to the same address above — include your device model and iOS version if you're reporting something that isn't working as expected.
