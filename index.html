Features

Everything a sideline broadcast needs

No production truck, no laptop, no second operator — it all runs on the phone doing the filming.

01

Direct to YouTube, live

Streams straight from the camera to a scheduled YouTube broadcast — no relay server, no separate encoder box.

02

Real scoreboard overlay

Team names, logos, colors, live score, and the game clock are burned directly into the video viewers see — not a browser overlay layered on top.

03

Added time & event cards

Fouls, yellow cards, red cards, offside, and stoppage time all get their own on-screen callouts, triggered right from the sideline.

04

HD through 4K

Pick a resolution and that's exactly what's delivered — no hidden downscaling. Bitrate adjusts itself automatically to match your connection.

05

Smooth zoom control

A dedicated on-screen zoom stick sweeps across every lens on the phone, so following the play stays smooth instead of a jump-cut pinch.

06

Built-in scheduling

Create the matchup — teams, colors, logos, kickoff time — and it syncs straight to YouTube's own broadcast scheduling. Nothing to re-enter on game day.
